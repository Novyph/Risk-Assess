Probability* — Review Past Incidents
1No incidents in last 3 years — task appears well-managed
2One incident in last 3 years
3One incident in last 2 years — more recent history raises risk
4More than one incident in last 2 years — hazard is actively causing harm
Severity* — Worst Credible Outcome
1First aid only — treatable on-site, no medical referral
2Medical attention or lost time — clinic/ER or restricted duty
3Long-term illness or serious injury — lasting consequences, surgery
4Death or permanent disability — if the energy source is capable of it
Control* — Hierarchy of Controls (0 = best)
0Elimination — hazard removed entirely (RPI = 0)
1Substitution — safer alternative in place
2Engineering controls — guards, ventilation, interlocks
3Administrative controls — SOPs, signage, rotation
4PPE — does not eliminate the hazard
5Behavior only — no formal control. Action required.
